About Us

Mishu — A story of two cities and one family

Our story begins in Antwerp, where the diamond trade is a language and the market a classroom. There, two generations of our family learned to read a rough stone like a musician reads a score — sensing the hidden note of brilliance and knowing where to cut so it will sing. Those early years taught us patience, respect for provenance, and the quiet confidence that comes from doing difficult work well.

When markets shifted, we carried that knowledge to Mumbai. The move was more than geographic; it was a choice to combine Antwerp's exacting standards with India's extraordinary craftsmanship. In Mumbai we built a workshop where planning is as important as polish, and every large rough is mapped, measured, and imagined before the first cut.

The craft we practice
Large natural rough diamonds demand more than tools — it demands judgment. For stones 10.80 carats and above we create bespoke cutting plans that balance weight retention, optical performance, and market value. Each stage — assessment, cleaving or sawing, bruting, and final polish — is documented and executed with the same care our founders used at the trading table.

What guides us
We are guided by three commitments: heritage (the lessons of Antwerp), precision (the discipline of the bench), and integrity (transparent provenance and certification). These commitments shape how we source, how we cut, and how we present every diamond to our clients.

Craft and Process

How a large stone becomes a story

Assessment — We begin with a careful reading of the rough: grain, inclusions, and potential
Planning — A bespoke cutting plan balances weight, brilliance, and market value
Execution — Sawing, bruting, and polishing by experienced hands with constant quality checks
Certification — Where appropriate, stones are submitted for GIA, HRD, or IGI grading
Presentation — Final stones are photographed, catalogued, and offered with full documentation
Why this matters
Large natural diamond stones are rare and complex. Thoughtful planning preserves value and reveals the stone's best light. Our process is designed to protect both beauty and investment.

Aiko Diamonds

Aiko Diamonds was born at the workbench where Mishu cutters and designers met to imagine what a great stone could become. What began as a conversation about a single exceptional polished diamond grew into an atelier that turns those stones into personal stories — engagement rings that begin new chapters, necklaces that hold memories, and collector pieces that celebrate rarity. Each commission is treated as a quiet collaboration: we listen to the stone, learn the client's intent, and shape a design that lets the diamond speak.

Our process is intimate and deliberate. From the first private consultation to CAD previews and the final hand finish, Aiko Diamonds blends technical mastery with refined design so every piece becomes an heirloom.

We create bespoke pieces set with natural polished diamonds: engagement rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and limited collector editions. We also remount and restore inherited stones, giving them new life while honoring their history.
